About Me

Hello! I am currently a student enrolled in a Trilogy Education Services Full Stack Web Development Boot Camp. I currently hold a B.S. in Psychology and a certificate for teaching English in foreign countries. Previously, I was working with children who have special needs - I am a Registered Behavior Technician(RBT) through the Behavior Analysts Certification Board. I am based in Saint Paul, MN. When I'm not working or coding, I enjoy rock climbing, throwing together cosplays for nerd conventions, and hanging out with my two cats.
